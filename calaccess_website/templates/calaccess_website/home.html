Who are we?

The California Civic Data Coalition is an open-source team of journalists and computer programmers from news organizations across America. Read more »

What do we do?

We create software that makes California's public data easier to analyze. Our current focus is CAL-ACCESS, the database tracking money in state politics. Download the data »

How can I get involved?

Our work is the result of collobration between hundreds of developers and journalists at dozens of different news organizations. We want you to be the next to contribute.

Fork our code on GitHub, reach out by e-mail or chime in on Slack.
