What is this?

Welcome to the open-source archive of campaign finance and lobbying disclosure data from the California Secretary of State's CAL-ACCESS database.

This site is maintained by the California Civic Data Coalition, an open-source team of journalists and computer programmers from news organizations across America.

What's next?

We're working on "processed data" downloads that will refine the CAL-ACCESS data and format it in simpler models that are easier to analyze.

How can I reach out?

You can reach us on Twitter or email us at californiacivicdata@gmail.com.
